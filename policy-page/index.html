Introduction & Our Commitment

Welcome to WowWraps. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your personal information. This policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection when you visit our restaurant, use our website, or engage with our services.

At WowWraps, we believe that exceptional food and service go hand in hand with respect for your privacy. We have designed our data practices to be straightforward, secure, and centered on your needs as our valued customer.

By using our website or services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide you with the best possible dining experience and to improve our services. The types of information we gather include:

Personal Information

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when you make reservations or place orders.
  • Account Information: Username, password, and preferences if you create an account on our platform.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, and transaction history processed securely through our payment partners.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent on our website, menu items browsed, and interaction patterns.
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address to show relevant restaurant locations and delivery options.

Customer Feedback & Interactions

  • Reviews, ratings, and comments you submit about our food and service.
  • Communications with our customer support team via email, phone, or chat.
  • Responses to surveys, promotions, or marketing campaigns.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and understand how you interact with our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and improve our services.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for basic website functionality, security, and navigation. Cannot be disabled. Session
Functional Cookies Remember your preferences like language, theme, and location for a personalized experience. 1 Year
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors use our website, which pages are popular, and where improvements are needed. 2 Years
Marketing Cookies Track your visit across our website to deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness. 90 Days

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect your ability to use some features of our website. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or alert you when new cookies are being sent.

Data Security & Protection

Protecting your personal information is a top priority at WowWraps. We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Our Security Measures

  • Encryption: All sensitive data, including payment information, is transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption protocols.
  • Secure Storage: Personal data is stored on secure servers with restricted access and regular security audits.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel with legitimate business needs can access your information.
  • Regular Updates: Our systems are regularly updated with the latest security patches and improvements.
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for suspicious activities and potential security threats.

Data Breach Protocol

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours and provide details about the incident, the data affected, and steps we're taking to address the situation. We will also inform relevant regulatory authorities as required by law.

While we strive to protect your information, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection.
